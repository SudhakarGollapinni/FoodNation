Aalu Mutter

Aalu Mutter is a preparation of Potatoes and Green Peas in a thick gravy made up of cream, spinach and spices. It is mainly eaten with roti, chappati or other breads.





Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Green Peas 1 cup
  • Onion ,quartered 2 large
  • Oil 2 tablespoons
  • Cloves 3
  • Black peppercorns 4-5
  • Cinnamon 2 one inch sticks
  • Bay leaf 1
  • Green chillies,slit 2
  • Ginger paste 1 teaspoon
  • Garlic paste 1 teaspoon
  • Cashewnut paste 1/4 cup
  • Yogurt 1/2 cup
  • Cream 1/2 cup
  • Saffron (kesar) a pinch
  • Garam masala powder 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt to taste
  • Green cardamom powder 1/4 teaspoon
